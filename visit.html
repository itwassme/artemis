Artemis is a superfood grocer and luncheonette that’s great for hanging out or taking out. We’re mostly inspired by the Italian hoagie shops, Jewish delis, and Greek diners of old-school Philadelphia, but we’re down for whatever. And most of our food—from meats, to condiments, to jams, and pickles—is made in house. So come on in for eggs, bagels, and barista made coffee in the AM, or house-corned-beef sandwiches, vegan hoagies, and colorful salads for lunch. We’re really stoked to have you. Really, we are.

Pickup and delivery only for now! But we love and miss having you here!!!
